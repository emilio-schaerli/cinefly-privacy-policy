# Privacy Policy for CineFly Forecast

**Effective Date:** May 15, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the CineFly Forecast app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Emilio as a Free service. This service is provided "AS IS".

### Information Collection and Use

The Application does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide personal identifiable information (such as your name or email address) to function. 

To provide its core features—specifically fetching real-time weather conditions, cloud cover, and UV index to recommend optimal ND filters and camera settings—the Application requires access to your device's **Location Data**. 

* **Location Data:** The Application uses your location solely to request local weather data from our third-party weather provider. This data is not stored on any external servers owned by the developer, nor is it sold or shared with advertising networks.

### Third-Party Access

Only aggregated, anonymized data is periodically transmitted to external services to help the developer improve the Application and its service. The Application utilizes third-party services that have their own Privacy Policies about handling data. 

Below are the links to the Privacy Policies of the third-party service providers used by the Application:
* [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* [Open-Meteo API](https://open-meteo.com/en/features#terms)

### Security

We value your trust in providing us with your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
